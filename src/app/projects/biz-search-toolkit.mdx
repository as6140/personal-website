## Overview

A fully automated deal-sourcing system covering two acquisition strategies: small business acquisitions (BizBuySell, Acquire.com) and commercial real estate (Crexi). Listings are scraped via headless browser, scored against a strict buy box using hard filters and a 0–100 soft scoring engine, enriched by Claude AI for qualitative analysis, and synced to Google Sheets — leaving only ranked, actionable deals for human review.

## Buy Boxes

### Small Business Acquisitions

Targets B2B, recurring-revenue businesses priced $1.2M–$2.0M with SDE $400K–$700K and an SDE multiple ≤ 3.25x. Hard-rejects anything B2C-heavy, owner-led on sales, or requiring physical presence.

### Commercial Real Estate (Crexi)

Evaluates self-storage, multifamily, retail, industrial, and other property types on Cap Rate, Cash-on-Cash Return, and DSCR — computed from configurable financing assumptions (LTV, interest rate, amortization period).

## How It Works

1. **Scrape** — Playwright headless Chrome captures live listings from BizBuySell, Acquire.com, and Crexi, including dynamically rendered content
2. **Score** — A two-stage engine applies hard filters (auto-reject) then a 0–100 soft score across Financial Quality, Business Model, Operational Independence, and Remote Compatibility
3. **Enrich** — Claude (Haiku) adds recurring revenue estimates, owner-dependency flags, business model classification, and a score adjustment for PASS deals
4. **Sync** — Results write to Google Sheets across four color-coded tabs (green = CONTACT, yellow = WATCH, grey = IGNORE): Deals, Scoring Breakdown, CRM Pipeline, and CRE Deals

## Built-in CRM Pipeline

A lightweight CLI-driven CRM tracks active deals through stages (CONTACTED → UNDER_NDA → LOI → DUE_DILIGENCE) with timestamped notes, follow-up date alerts, and stage filtering — all without leaving the terminal.

## Technologies Used

- **Python** — core pipeline, scoring engine, and CLI
- **Playwright** — headless scraping for JS-rendered listing pages
- **Claude API (Haiku)** — LLM enrichment for qualitative business analysis
- **Google Sheets API** — output storage and CRM sync across four tabs
- **SQLite** — local deduplication and intermediate pipeline state
- **Click** — CLI interface with 20+ commands covering scrape, score, enrich, sync, and CRM operations
